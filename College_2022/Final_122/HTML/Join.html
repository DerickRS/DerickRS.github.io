BECOME A MEMBER OF THE KANSAS CITY GUITAR SOCIETY


If you enjoy our concerts and would like to help support our efforts or are interested in the guitar, become a member of the Kansas City Guitar Society. Memberships are available for as little as $25!

Membership support helps guarantee classical guitar programming throughout the year. Without our loyal supporters, we couldn’t have survived for over 30 years.

Thanks for believing in the Kansas City Guitar Society’s mission, and for your love of this beautiful instrument and its music. Playing the guitar is not a requirement for membership, only a love for the music and the instrument is needed!
