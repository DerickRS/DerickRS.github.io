Casual Concert Series

We Have A New Location
Colonial Church in Prairie Village 7039 Mission Road Prairie Village, KS 66208
Our Casual Concerts are held on Sunday afternoons in the Sanctuary of the Colonial Church in Prairie Village.
Featuring local and regional guitarists and musicians, these concerts are held on Sunday afternoons from 3:30-4:30.
Concerts are open to the public at no cost. They are FREE. The Kansas City Guitar Society pays all performers for their artistry from the generous donations of our supporters. Donations are gratefully received!

Upcoming Events:

Nilko Andreas, Classical Guitar and Christine Grossman, Violist

Since his Carnegie Hall solo debut in 2009, multi-faceted touring artist Nilko Andreas has been performing on three continents as an internationally awarded classical guitarist, Composer, Actor, Music Director, and singer (La Cumbiamba eNeYe, LaMar NYC), Born in Colombia, he carries the musical legacy of his motherland, layered with his experiences as a leading classical musician around the world and 22 years of living, producing and conducting in New York City where he constantly performs as a soloist and well as Chamber Musician with different ensembles.

Daniel Krieg In Concert

Daniel Krieg currently serves as the Vice-President of the Kansas City Guitar society. While his career path lead him to work as a Software Engineer, his undergraduate studies with Muriel Anderson at Wheaton College Conservatory of Music and graduate studies with Douglas Niedt at the University of Missouri at Kansas City enabled him to craft the technique and artistry to present enjoyable and unique musical selections.
